Who is me?

Born in Stamford Connecticut, I moved across the pond to London when I was at the tender age of 2. After 8 years as a die-hard Gunner, we relocated back across the atlantic to Tribeca, New York. Four Years Ago, after many misdiagnoses following a spell of mono and pneumonia, I was diagnosed with POTS, an autonomic heart condition that slows down my heart rate. My symptoms include chronic pain, exhaustion, headaches, chronic fatigue and IBS. It has taken me some time, but four years later, I am the best I have ever been. I play soccer, train Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, bike, lift, and do yoga. On top of this, I'm gluten free, dairy free, and red-meat free.
